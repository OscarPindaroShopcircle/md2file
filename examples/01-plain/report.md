# Circeus operating overview

Circeus acquires and operates software companies for the long term. This overview
summarises how we work, what we look for, and how a partnership unfolds — written
as plain content, with no styling applied.

## What we do

We partner with founders who have built something durable and want a responsible
home for it. We are operators, not flippers: we hold, invest, and grow.

## Operating principles

1. Founder-first ownership — we honour the team and product that already exist.
2. Long-term hold — no arbitrary exit clock, no strip-and-sell.
3. Grayscale over noise — clarity and focus beat vanity metrics.
4. Compounding, not shortcuts — steady reinvestment over quick wins.

## What we look for

- Profitable or near-profitable software businesses.
- A loyal customer base and low churn.
- A product the team is proud of.
- An owner who wants continuity for customers and staff.

## How a partnership works

| Stage        | What happens                          | Typical time |
|:-------------|:--------------------------------------|:------------:|
| Intro        | A short, no-pressure conversation     | 1 week       |
| Diligence    | We learn the business, you learn us   | 2–4 weeks    |
| Offer        | A clear, fair structure               | 1 week       |
| Transition   | We take on operations, you choose role| Ongoing      |
